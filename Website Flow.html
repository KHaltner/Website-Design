Goals & Success

Let's first talk about the why behind the site you want to create. Defining the goal or objective of the site is the first step in creating a site.
What is the goal / objective of this site?
  • To sell something?
  • To information to current and potential clients?
  • To promote and market your services?
  • To provider easier access to services?
  • To allow customers to interact with your organization?
  • Other?
  • Don't know?

Scope & Deliverables

Now that you know why you're creating a site it's time to define the what of the site. This is also referred to as scope.
Scope definition
Requires defining the area to which is relevant in achieving the goal.
Tips:
Make sure your targets are Specific, Measureable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-Oriented (SMART).
Consider
  • How will you measure success?
  • Whom do you envision as your core users?
  • What do you want those users to gain by visiting the site?
  • What do you want users to do while they're on the site?
  • What do you want the users to do after they're on your site?
  • How does your idea for a website compare with others?
  • Do you expect to make money on your website?
  • Will your site need to collect input from the user?
